# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for all contributors and participants in this project.

We aim to foster an open, inclusive, and collaborative space focused on building reliable and high-quality software.

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication
- Provide constructive feedback and engage in good faith discussions
- Focus on technical collaboration and problem solving
- Be open to different perspectives and ideas

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Harassment, intimidation, or abusive language
- Personal attacks or insulting comments
- Discriminatory or exclusionary behavior
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- Any public or private communication related to the project

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior, including:

- Warning the contributor
- Removing or editing content
- Rejecting contributions
- Temporarily or permanently restricting participation

All enforcement actions will be applied fairly and respectfully.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers.

Reports will be handled with discretion and care.
